Andrew Nguyen

Andrew Nguyen is a dedicated full-time student at the University of Houston (UH) and the proud owner of a flourishing cafeteria. With a passion for culinary arts deeply rooted in his family's restaurant business, Andrew has spent the past 16 years honing his skills and knowledge in cafeteria management. His journey began at a young age, working alongside his family in their restaurant, where he gained invaluable experience in the culinary world.

Andrew's ambition to pursue higher education while simultaneously running a business led him to invest his student loan money into establishing his own cafeteria. Andrew's commitment to excellence in cuisine has earned him recognition as a 1 Michelin star chef, a testament to his culinary expertise and dedication to quality. In 2023, his cafeteria achieved remarkable success, generating an impressive $10,000 in profit.
